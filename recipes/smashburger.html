Simple Smashburgers

A smashburger in a plaid fast food basket, along with some plain Ruffles.

Description

These incredibly easy smashburgers take just 20 minutes of time total for 4 burgers! Smashburgers are basically regular burger patties that have been smooshed very thin, and then stacked on top of each other with a slice of cheese inbetween. They're very juicy and flavorful because of the perfect sear that they get. You can cook these on a grill or on a pan, but I recommend doing it on a grill. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 2 tbsp. butter, softened
  • 1 lb. ground beef (80% lean)
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • Any burger toppings
  • Salt to taste
  • Chips of choice for side (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill on high head and lightly oil. Preheat a flat-top griddle on the grill until smoking.
  2. Spread butter on the inside of the buns and toast until browned. Set aside.
  3. Form meat into 8 loose-packed 2 oz. balls.
  4. Place the balls onto the flat-top griddle and smash until 0.25in. thick. Use two spatulas to smash. Sprinkle with salt.
  5. Grill for 45 seconds, until the edges are dark rown. Flip over the patties and cover 4 with cheese.
  6. Grill for another 15-20 seconds, then stack the un-cheesed ones on top of the cheesed ones. Move to a bun.
  7. Serve with whatever toppings you want and whatever chips you like!
