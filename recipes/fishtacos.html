Grilled Fish Tacos

Three fully dressed fish tacos on a grey plate.

Description

While it takes about 6 hours to marinate this fish, it's all worth it in the end for these juicy, flavorful fish tacos. You may use any toppings you like, however I reccomend cabbage, cilantro, lime juice, tomatoes and queso fresco or cotija. You will also make a delicious chipotle-lime crema to go on top. Enjoy!

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 1 container (8 oz.) light sour cream
  • 0.5 cups adobo sauce from chipotle peppers
  • 2 tbsp. fresh lime juice
  • 2 tsp. lime zest
  • 0.5 tsp. seafood seasoning (like old bay)
  • 0.25 tsp. cumin
  • 0.25 tsp. chili powder
  • Salt and pepper

Fish

  • 0.25 cups extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp. distilled white vinegar
  • 2 tbsp. fresh lime juice
  • 2 tsp. lime zest
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1.5 tsp. honey
  • 1 tsp. seafood seasoning (like old bay)
  • 1 tsp. hot sauce, or to taste
  • 0.5 tsp. cumin
  • 0.5 tsp chili powder
  • 0.5 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 lb. tilapia filet, cut into chunks

Assembly

  • Tortillas of choice
  • 3 ripe tomatoes, seeded, diced
  • 1 head of cabbage, cored, shredded
  • 1 bunch cilantro, chopped
  • 2 limes, cut into wedges
  • 1 container queso fresco or cotija

Steps

Marinated Fish

  1. Whisk together olive oil, vinegar, lime juice, lime zest, garlic, honey, seafood seasoning, hot pepper sauce, cumin, chili powder, and black pepper until blended.
  2. Place tilapia in a dish and pour over marinade. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 6 hours.

Dressing

  1. Mix together sour cream and adobo sauce in a bowl. Stir in lime juice, zest, and seasonings. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Cover and refrigerate until plating.

Cooking and Plating

  1. Preheat a grill to high and lightly oil. Set grate 4 inches from heat.
  2. Remove fish from marinade, drain and discard excess marinade. Grill fish until easily flaked with a fork, turning once, about 9 minutes.
  3. Place fish in the center of tortillas, top with tomatoes, cabbage, cilantro, and cheese to taste.
  4. Drizzle with dressing and garnish with lime wedges.
