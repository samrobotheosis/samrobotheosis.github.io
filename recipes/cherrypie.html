Sour Cherry Pie

A finished sour cherry pie with a lattice pattern.

Description

This easy pie recipe takes just 10 minutes of prep time, so it's great if you're in
a rush. If you've never had them before, sour cherries are not quite as sweet as
regular cherries, which adds an amazing tartness to the filling. While it's not required,
I would recommend adding a scoop of vanilla ice cream to balance the sourness. Feel
free to make your own pie crust or to get it store-bought; either one works. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 15 oz (one package) ready-to-use pie crust
  • 3 cans pitted sour cherries, drained
  • 3 tbsp. quick acting tapioca
  • 1.5 cups white sugar
  • 0.25 tsp almond extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Press one pie crust into a 9 inch pie plate.
  3. Mix together cherries, tapoica, sugar, and almond extract in a bowl and let stand for 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Cut another pie crust into half inch strips. Pour the cherry mixture into the pie dish,
    and arrange the strips into a lattice pattern, and pinch the edges to seal. Lattice making tutorial
  5. Bake for 40 to 50 minutes or until the crust is golden brown. Cool before serving.
